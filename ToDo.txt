For anyone else wondering about the nav-link Historia font, here are the steps:
1. Download the font at https://www.dafont.com/the-historia.font
2. Upload TheHistoriaDemo.ttf here to generate a web font kit https://www.fontsquirrel.com/tools/webfont-generator
3. From the kit, take .woff and .woff2 files and paste them in your project folder. I created a subfolder fonts for that.
4. Put this in your CSS: 
@font-face {
  font-family: 'Marlon';
  src: url('fonts/marlon-webfont.woff2') format('woff2'),
       url('fonts/marlon-webfont.woff') format('woff');
  font-weight: normal;
  font-style: normal;
}

.nav-link{
  font-family:"Marlon", sans-serif;
}
